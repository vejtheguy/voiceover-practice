Lesson 3: Scrolling

When VoiceOver is on, scrolling up and down a webpage changes from the typical single finger scrolling. To scroll down, use three fingers together and swipe up. To scroll back up, use three fingers together and swipe down.

Task: Try Scrolling

With VoiceOver on, use three fingers to scroll down the page to find more content. If scrolling isn't working, make sure you have selected an element within the actual webpage, rather than an item in the browser's top tabs or toolbars.
