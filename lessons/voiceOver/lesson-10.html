Lesson 10: Using the Rotor Tool (Part 2)

To actually use your selected item, you must swipe up or down with one finger. I find that it works best if you do this as a quick, sharp flick.

Turn the Rotor to "Words." Select and focus on the sentence below, and practice flicking down to read it one single word at a time. Don't forget you can also flick upwards to read back up.

Note: Swiping left or right continues to read element-by-element across the page. To utilize the selected option from the rotor wheel, use a single upward or downward flick.
