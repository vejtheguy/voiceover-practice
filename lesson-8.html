Lesson 8: Jump to Top or Bottom

A very effective way to quickly navigate to the top of the page or bottom of the page is by using a four finger tap.

Navigate to the Top

To quickly get to the top of the page, use four fingers together and tap towards the top quarter of the screen. This will make the screen reader jump its focus to the very first item on the page in one simple move.

Try this gesture now. You will see that the screen reader jumps its focus to the first item on the page.

Using the Google Chrome browser, this is the very first tab in the tabs section for me.

Navigate to the Bottom

To quickly get to the bottom of the page, use four fingers together and tap towards the bottom quarter of the screen. This will make the screen reader jump its focus to the very last item on the page in one simple move.

Try this gesture now. You will see that the screen reader jumps its focus to the bottom of the webpage.

This will be the last item of the footer section on this webpage.
