Resize Text (Level AA)

Official Guideline: WCAG 1.4.4
The WCAG Goal:

Ensure text is adaptive and resizable up to 200% (doubled in size) without loss of content, functionality, or breaking the layout structure.

My Implementation:

I was able to achieve this by utilizing rem units for all of my font-sizing rather than fixed px values. This ensures that the text properly scales dynamically based on the root element (<html>).
