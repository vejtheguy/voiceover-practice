Opening Links

Opening Links in New Tabs (or Not)
My Implementation:

I have always created links on my webpages using target="_blank" to open them in new tabs. For most screen readers though, this causes a problem and complexity for the user. To avoid this, I have left links to open in the same tab by default. I believe this issue is tied to WCAG 3.2.5, where users should have full control of major content changes.

Reflow (Level AA)

Official Guideline: WCAG 1.4.10
The WCAG Goal:

Content can be enlarged without increasing line length. There is no loss of information or functionality, and without requiring scrolling in two dimensions.

My Implementation:

I achieved this by primarily using rem units for all of my font-sizing and layout spacing. I also used display flex for my layout structure, allowing me to make sure content is responsive and reflows properly when the viewport size changes.

Contrast (Enhanced) (Level AAA)

Official Guideline: WCAG 1.4.6
The WCAG Goal:

Visual presentation of text has a contrast ratio of at least 7:1, or large-scale text have contrast ratio 4.5:1.

My Implementation:

I believe I have achieved this across all of the text content on this website. This WCAG goal is just a much more intense version of the previous contrast requirement, which I have met well above the 7:1 ratio on most things. Some of the blue text on gray background is lower, but I think because of the font-size being larger, I am still meeting the requirement of 4.5:1 for large-scale text.

Contrast (Minimum) (Level AA)

Official Guideline: WCAG 1.4.3
The WCAG Goal:

Visual presentation of text has a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1, or large-scale text have contrast ratio 3:1.

My Implementation:

I utilized the Google Chrome extension Colour Contrast Checker to analyze the contrast ratio of my text and background colors. This actually made it easier for me to choose color combinations as I was designing the website. For example, this color text is a very dark gray (#191919) on a white background (#ffffff), giving me a contrast ration of 17.58:1, which is well above the minimum requirement of 4.5:1.

Resize Text (Level AA)

Official Guideline: WCAG 1.4.4
The WCAG Goal:

Ensure text is adaptive and resizable up to 200% (doubled in size) without loss of content, functionality, or breaking the layout structure.

My Implementation:

I was able to achieve this by utilizing rem units for all of my font-sizing rather than fixed px values. This ensures that the text properly scales dynamically based on the root element (<html>).
