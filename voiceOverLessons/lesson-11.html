Lesson 11: Navigating Features

A handy gesture to learn early is the single-finger slide up from the bottom of the screen to navigate to the Home Screen or App Switcher. Sound is your friend here.

Navigate to Home Screen

Using one finger, slide up from the bottom of the iPad slowly. Listen closely, as you want to hear two notes play. This is your indicator that you have selected the option to go back to the Home Screen.

Try going to the Home Screen now. Then, practice navigating back to this webpage by either selecting it in the dock at the bottom of the screen, or navigating to your browser app on the Home Screen.

Tip: When on the Home Screen, you can slide left or right with three fingers to navigate between different pages.

App Switcher

Using one finger, slide up from the bottom of the iPad slowly. Listen closely, as you want to hear three notes play. This is the indicator that you have selected the App Switcher, which will display all of your currently open apps. Swipe right or left on this screen to navigate between them.

Try using the App Switcher now. If you have another app open, try navigating to it and opening it by double-tapping the screen once it is selected. Then, use the App Switcher again to navigate back to this webpage.

Note: You can close apps inside the App Switcher by selecting an app and using a three-finger slide up gesture.
