Lesson 1: Activating Items

In order to click on something using VoiceOver, double tap anywhere on the screen when you have it selected.

Split-tap Gesture

Another useful way to activate an item is to touch and hold the item you want to click with one finger, and while that is being held, tap another finger down on the screen.

Try clicking the button below using the split-tap gesture.
