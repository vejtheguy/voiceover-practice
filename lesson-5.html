Lesson 5: Continuous Reading (Read All)

A useful gesture to utilize with VoiceOver is the "Read All" feature. This tells VoiceOver to automatically read all content on the page without you needing to manually navigate item by item.

Read Entire Screen

Using two fingers, do a swipe up gesture on the screen. VoiceOver will move the cursor to the very top of the screen and read through everything from the top downward.

Try the gesture now. You will see that the screen reader starts at the very top of the browser, reading through the tabs and address bar, and then down through this webpage continuously.

Read Screen from Current Focus

Using two fingers, do a swipe down gesture on the screen. VoiceOver will begin reading from where your current focus is on the page.

Select this text on the screen and try the two finger down swipe gesture.

The screen reader should automatically read its way down to the bottom of the screen, skipping the content above.

Tip: Using the Pause Feature

If you are utilizing the Read All feature and ever need to pause, use a two finger single tap on the screen. This will pause the screen reader. You can then do the same two-finger single tap again to resume reading right where you left off.
