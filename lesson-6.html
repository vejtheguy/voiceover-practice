Lesson 6: Go Back or Escape

This gesture is useful for when you need to close a pop-up or sub-menu. You can think of it as the back button or escape key.

Scrub Gesture

Using two fingers together, scrub back and forth three times on the screen.

Best if done in a Z shape. To do this, slide from left to right, then diagonaly down to the left, and then slide to the right again.

Scrub: Exit the Pop-up

Click on the button below and a pop-up will appear on the screen.

Use the two finger scrub gesture to close out of the pop-up.

Pop-up Practice

This is a pop-up box. Use the two finger scrub gesture to close it.

Tip: If using a keyboard, use the Escape key to close this.
