Lesson 0: Starting VoiceOver

Turn on VoiceOver to practice through these lessons. Having a quick way to turn it off is also important in case you get stuck.

Quick Shortcut: Triple Click

Quickly click the top power button three times consecutively. This will turn VoiceOver on.

If you get stuck or want to stop using VoiceOver, do the same move and it will turn VoiceOver off.

Manual Way: Settings Menu

  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Select Accessibility.
  3. Select VoiceOver at the very top.
  4. Toggle the switch to On.

Voice: Ask Siri

If you have Siri activated, VoiceOver can be turned on or off by stating "Turn on VoiceOver" or "Turn off VoiceOver".

Tip: If you have VoiceOver on, tap the link below once, and then double tap the screen anywhere to click it.
